How Work Side Works

Our platform is designed to be simple, efficient, and secure. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to use Work Side as a Student.

For Students (Clients)

  1. Choose a Plan: Get started by selecting a plan that fits your needs.
  2. Post a Project:
    • Click on "Create a New Project."
    • Fill in the details: subject, deadline, page count, and specific instructions.
    • Upload any necessary files (rubrics, notes, etc.).
  3. Fund Your Project: Add funds to your wallet using our secure payment gateways (UPI, Card, etc.). The funds for your project are held in escrow until you approve the work.
  4. A Worker is Assigned: Once your project is funded, a verified Worker will be assigned to your project and will begin working.
  5. Communicate: Use the built-in chat to communicate with the User. Answer any questions they have to ensure the best results.
  6. Review & Approve: You will receive a notification when the work is delivered. Review the file, and if you are satisfied, click "Approve" to release the payment to the User.
