Gumasta License

A Gumasta License is a registration required for doing any kind of business in the state of Maharashtra. It is governed by the Municipal Corporation of the respective area. Our service helps you to obtain a Gumasta License without any hassle.

Required Documents for Gumasta License

1. Essential Documents (For All Businesses)

  • Identity Proof: Aadhaar Card or PAN Card of the owner/authorized person.
  • Address Proof of Business:
    • If Owned: Latest Property Tax Receipt, Sale Deed, or Electricity Bill.
    • If Rented: Registered Rent Agreement AND a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the landlord.
  • Shop Photo: A clear photo of the shop/office from the outside showing the Name Board in Marathi (Devnagri script). Note: The Marathi font size must not be smaller than any other language on the board.
  • Owner’s Photo & Signature: Scanned passport-size photograph and a clear signature on white paper.

2. Entity-Specific Documents

Business TypeAdditional Documents Required

  • Sole Proprietorship: Self-declaration form (available on the portal).
  • Partnership Firm: Partnership Deed and PAN Card of the firm.
  • Pvt Ltd / OPC: Certificate of Incorporation (COI) and MOA/AOA.
  • LLP: LLP Agreement.
  • Trust/Society: Copy of the Trust Deed or Society Bylaws.

3. Employee Details (Important)

The "Gumasta" name is often used for businesses with 10 or more employees. If you have fewer than 10 employees, you only need to file an Intimation (Form F).

  • Employee List: Names, gender, and nature of work for all staff.
  • Wages/Hours: A declaration of working hours and weekly holidays.

4. Quick Checklist for Uploading

Pro-Tip: Government portals are very strict about file sizes.

  • Photos/Signature: Usually between 5 KB and 20 KB.
  • ID & Address Proofs: Usually between 75 KB and 100 KB in PDF or JPEG.

Why do you need Gumasta?

  • Bank Account: You cannot open a "Current Account" in the name of your business in Maharashtra without this.
  • GST Link: It serves as primary proof of address for your GST application.
  • Legal Safety: It prevents heavy fines during municipal inspections.
