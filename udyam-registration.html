Udyam Registration

Udyam Registration, also known as MSME Registration, is a government registration for micro, small, and medium enterprises. This registration provides your business with a unique identity and access to various government schemes and benefits.

Our service helps you to get your Udyam Registration certificate quickly and easily. We handle all the paperwork and online procedures for you.

Mandatory Requirements

These are the absolute essentials without which you cannot proceed:

1. Mandatory Requirements

  • Aadhaar Number:
    • For Proprietorship: Aadhaar of the proprietor.
    • For Partnership: Aadhaar of the Managing Partner.
    • For Company/LLP: Aadhaar of the Authorized Signatory.
  • PAN Card: PAN of the organization (or the proprietor's PAN for a single-owner business). PAN is now mandatory for Udyam registration.
  • Active Mobile Number: To receive the OTP (One-Time Password) linked to your Aadhaar.

2. Business & Banking Details

You will need to enter these specific details into the portal:

  • Business Address: Complete address of the plant/unit and the registered office (Village, District, State, PIN Code).
  • Bank Account Details: Bank Account Number and IFSC Code of the branch.
  • Date of Commencement: The exact date when your business officially started operating.
  • Investment & Turnover Details: Previous year's Investment in Plant & Machinery and Total Turnover (Gross sales) for the previous financial year.

3. Classification Details (NIC Codes)

You must identify your business category. The portal uses the National Industrial Classification (NIC) system to categorize your business.

  • Primary Activity: Whether you are Manufacturing or Service.
  • NIC 2-digit, 4-digit, and 5-digit codes: These represent your specific niche (e.g., "Manufacture of apparel" or "IT Services").
