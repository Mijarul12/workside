JanSamarth Loan Assistance

The JanSamarth portal is a one-stop digital platform linking over a dozen government credit schemes. Our service helps you navigate the complexities of the application process, ensuring you have all the correct documents and information ready for submission.

Required Documents for JanSamarth Loan Application

To apply for a loan on the JanSamarth portal, you need a specific set of documents. The requirements vary based on the loan you are applying for.

1. Mandatory Personal Documents

  • Aadhaar Number: Must be linked to your active mobile number for OTP verification.
  • PAN Card: Mandatory for business and personal loan tracking.
  • Voter ID / Passport: (Optional, as secondary ID proof).
  • Caste Certificate: If you are applying under categories like SC/ST/OBC to get higher subsidies.

2. Business & Income Proofs

  • Udyam Registration Certificate: Almost all business loans on JanSamarth require you to be registered as an MSME.
  • Bank Statement: Usually for the last 6 to 12 months (in PDF format).
  • Income Tax Returns (ITR): Last 2–3 years of ITR (if applicable).
  • GST Registration details: If your business is GST-registered.

3. Loan-Specific Documents

Depending on the loan category, you will need additional documents:

PMEGP (Business Loan)

  • Project Report: A detailed report on what you will do with the money.
  • Educational Certificate: 8th pass or higher for high-value loans.
  • Rural Area Certificate: (If applicable).

Education Loan

  • Admission Letter: From the College/University.
  • Fee Structure.
  • Student's Marksheets: 10th/12th/Graduation.

Agricultural Loan

  • Land Records: (Khasra/Khatauni).
  • Details of Crops/Equipment being purchased.

Livelihood Loan

  • Self-Help Group (SHG) Registration certificate or Member ID.

4. Digital Requirements for the Portal

Before you log in to jansamarth.in, ensure you have:

  • Mobile Phone: Keep it nearby for multiple OTPs.
  • Digital Project Report: For business loans, the portal will ask for your estimated project cost (Machine cost + Working capital).
  • Bank Login/Net Banking: To facilitate "Bank Statement Finishing" (the portal often pulls your bank data directly if you give permission).

How the Process Works

JanSamarth doesn't give you the money directly; it gives you "In-Principle Approval."

  1. Check Eligibility: You answer a few questions on the portal.
  2. Digital Documents: You provide the details mentioned above.
  3. Select Bank: You choose which bank you want to apply to from a list of lenders.
  4. Digital Approval: You get an approval letter which you then take to the bank branch to get the actual cash.
